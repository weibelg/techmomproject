INTRODUCTION
*WE START TRACKING ON MARCH 7, 2022!*

READ THIS EXPLANATION, and WELCOME!
This group is a support group where we encourage each other to keep healthy habits and fitness goals.

GUIDES - Just under the Facebook group picture you see the button for GUIDES. Each week there will be a new GUIDE with discussion topics. The guide is just a collection of posts, but the guide keeps them organized so you know you haven't missed anything.

PAY-IN: There are 2 ways to participate: Free or Paid.

FREE: You're welcome to just hang out in the group and learn some things. Warning- we have done this group ongoing for years, and only a very rare person gets much out of the free group.

PAID: Everyone who wants some ACCOUNTABILITY from the group pays $25 to the moderator, through venmo. We have found through iterations that people care more, and try harder, when they have invested a little something into a venture.

Those who pay will get a google doc TRACKER. The link is sent via FB messenger. **Since I'm a volunteer, I'm capping the group at 25 paid participants**

All who contribute are VOLUNTEERS who put this group together as a community service so that we, as friends, can all feel our best and take care of ourselves. Share the love!

TIMELINE: We will start tracking points on March 7 and end on May 1. That's 8 weeks.

At the end, you will get $15 prize if you A)Turn in a tracker every week AND ðŸ˜Ž Get 80% of your points.

The other $10 goes into a pot for a prize to top point earners.

COMPETITION: $10 from each of us goes into the pot, to be split by those with highest percentage of points for sticking to their good habits and doing the bonuses. This can be you!

TRACKING: In other groups on FB, everyone in the group tracks the SAME things. We want you to be able to tailor this to YOUR needs. So, YOU GET TO CHOOSE which things (minimum 5, no maximum) you will track.

You get to choose how many "points" you can earn each day for each item, and each week you will report your PERCENT. You cannot go over 100% each week.

You will also report a separate number of "bonus points". All of these numbers go together to determine contest winners.

Each SUNDAY by 8PM PST is the deadline for the week.

CHOICES: I'll post a sample tracker with too many choices on it. Once you join the group, you'll decide which things you DO and DON'T want to track for yourself.

Once you PAY $25 you will get your OWN TRACKER to edit. During the trial week, I will post several posts about why you may want to choose various things to track.

By March 7 you will have it locked down.

NAVIGATION: Posts that are labeled as 'announcements' in the Facebook Group can always be found again (listed under the Title of the Group, after 'Discussion' you have 'Announcements').

TOPICS will help you find posts that are Homework, Recipes, Introductions, Celebrations, Workouts by topic.

GUIDES: The weeks will be divided into GUIDES to help you go through the content. You should start going through this first Unit now, which helps you decide what you want to track! Each Sunday night the new Unit for the upcoming week will go live, and you can create a new week in your tracker using copy/paste.

If you want help with this step, you can get help (I have access to your tracker.) Plan to drop by this FB page regularly to see what discussion is going on. Homework units will be posted early in the week so you can choose to do it daily or all at once for the week.
