How It Works
Friends Supporting Each Other in Creating Healthy Habits

This is a group of friends keeping each other accountable. We keep it fun by tracking our goal-keeping and earning prizes. For now everything takes place in this FACEBOOK GROUP.
Nothing for Sale. Nothing to Buy

You will never see (or share) advertisements for any products. I reserve the right to pressure you to buy more vegetables, grains, and beans!
How Invested Are You?

Audit for free or pay $25 to play/compete. You'll care more, and try harder, with something invested!
Winning a Prize

$10 goes into the prize pot, but everyone who earns 80% points wins back the rest of their own money. My goal is to have everyone win. Note: LOSING WEIGHT is not a requirement for winning.
Your Personal Tracking Sheet

You'll create a personalized tracking sheet. YOU decide what is important to be working on. Eating well, moving your body, sleeping, creating order, music practice, education goals, special projects, and career goals might be included.
Science-Based Lifestyle Changes

No quick fixes, no miracle cures- we are here to establish lifestyle habits. Educational videos and articles will be posted for discussion. Join and help us become a team, learn strategies, and debunk misinformation.
Timeline

About

Hello, everybody! I'm your host, Gina Weibel.

Before transitioning into tech, I wore many hats in the community as both a private and in-school music teacher, high school sports coach, group fitness instructor, swim instructor, and mother of four.

During the 2020 shut-down, fitness classes stopped, swimming lessons stopped, high-school sports even stopped for a while. I really missed the camaraderie and accountability I get from coaching and being active!

I decided to start the Happy Healthy Habits group as a volunteer project for the community. It's my way to help all of us connect as we set and achieve goals.

I hold a Fitness Nutrition Specialist certification from ACE, a Group Fitness Specialist certification from ACE, and an M.S. from Cornell University. I love to get a little nerdy and hope you'll join me.

    - Gina Weibel
